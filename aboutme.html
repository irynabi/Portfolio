About Me

Iryn Abigail Delim is a multimedia designer studying at New York University's Tandon School of Engineering. With ample experience in web development, software applications, media marketing, and UX/UI, Iryn seeks to create user-friendly web applications that cater to underserved communities and enhance digital platforms for everyone.

As an intern for Media Art Xploration, Iryn delved into the intricacies of website management, creating dynamic web pages to intricately curate vital information in light of the organization’s complete rebranding. Her visual eye was crucial to the company’s redesign decisions from its conceptualization to final execution. Blending creativity with functionality, Iryn framed a seamless user navigation that maintains brand consistency and visual communication.

As Digital Design Co-Chair for the National Pre-Health Community, Iryn played a pivotal role in transforming the organization's web presence. Her work is present in revamping the website, designing graphics for promotional materials, such as business cards, to scripting and editing their 2023 conference promotional video. To expand the company’s impact to upcoming medical students and further their access to academic resources, she initiated communication with medical professionals, successfully securing partnerships and endorsements that bolstered the credibility of NPHC’s productions.

In her free time, Iryn loves to edit videos and produce all sorts of other media outlets, whether on TikTok, her friend’s Youtube channel, and their self-started podcasts. She takes pleasure in going to the gym and keeping up with all things fitness and health related. Iryn also has a fondness for baking, cooking, and exploring new culinary gems around her NYC area.
